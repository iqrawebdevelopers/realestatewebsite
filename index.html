Architectural
Philosophy

Natural Light

The plan was drawn around the sun's path, not around the walls. Deep roof overhangs filter harsh midday light while floor-to-ceiling glazing pulls the low morning and evening light deep into the interior.

Material Palette

Honed limestone, blackened steel, and bookmatched marble sit beside warm walnut millwork — a restrained set of materials repeated with discipline throughout, rather than a different finish in every room.

Indoor–Outdoor Living

Pocketing glass walls dissolve the boundary between the living room and the terrace, so the infinity pool reads as an extension of the floor plan rather than a separate feature.

Structural Engineering & Landscape

A cantilevered upper volume appears to float above the site, made possible by a hidden steel frame. Native, drought-tolerant planting and a geothermal system were part of the design brief from day one, not added afterward.
